## Setup

We are able to fax to any HIPAA-compliant fax number (either physical or virtual). Simply provide us with the number
you would like your documents delivered to.

<Warning>Delivery via fax is supported, however, it provides a lower level of experience and should be avoided when possible.</Warning>
